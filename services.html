FAQ

Questions We Get All the Time

Can't find your answer here? Drop us a message — we respond within 24 hours.

How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope. A marketing website typically takes 4–8 weeks. A SaaS MVP runs 12–20 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline during the discovery phase — and we stick to it.
Do you take on small projects?
Our minimum engagement is $8,000. We focus on projects where we can make a genuine impact — not one-page brochure sites. If your budget is smaller, we're happy to recommend other options.
Who owns the code when we're done?
You do — 100%. Upon final payment, all intellectual property, code, designs, and assets transfer fully to you. No licensing fees, no lock-in.
Can you work with our existing team?
Absolutely. We often embed alongside in-house teams, contributing specific expertise while respecting your existing stack, processes, and culture.
Do you offer maintenance and support?
Yes. Every project includes a free 30-day support window. We also offer ongoing retainer plans covering bug fixes, feature updates, security patches, and performance monitoring.
What's your payment structure?
Typically 30% upfront to begin, 40% at design/architecture sign-off, and 30% upon final delivery. For ongoing retainers, we invoice monthly in advance.
