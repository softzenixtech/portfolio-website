The Beginning

From a Two-Person Studio to a Global Tech Partner

Softzenix Tech started in 2018 when two software engineers — frustrated by agencies that over-promised and under-delivered — decided to build something different: a studio that treats every project as its own product.

We began with small web projects, then quickly earned a reputation for shipping clean, performant code on time. Word spread. Clients returned. The team grew — carefully, with purpose — until we became the full-stack digital partner we always set out to be.

Today, Softzenix Tech is a team of 20+ specialists across engineering, design, and product strategy. We've shipped products used by hundreds of thousands of people across six continents, and we're just getting started.
